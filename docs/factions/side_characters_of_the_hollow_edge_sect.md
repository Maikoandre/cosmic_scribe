# 🌀 Side Characters of the Hollow Edge Sect  
*Where space folds and silence cuts deeper than sound.*

> "In the Hollow Edge, even silence is sharp."

---

## 🧑‍🎓 Inner & Outer Disciples

### 🪞 **Xu Lingyin — Mirror-Bound Initiate**  
- **Rank**: Outer Disciple  
- **Specialty**: Reflective Sword Techniques & Minor Illusion Dao  
- **Personality**: Naïve but curious; obsessed with her own reflection.  
- **Notable Trait**: Her blade only cuts what she can see in her mirror shard.  

---

### ⚖️ **Fan Ruochan — Spatial Balancer**  
- **Rank**: Inner Disciple  
- **Specialty**: Equilibrium Steps & Folded Swordsmanship  
- **Personality**: Calm, meticulous, obsessed with symmetry.  
- **Notable Trait**: Can make uneven strikes feel equal in weight and force.  

---

### 🪶 **Chou Wen — Hollow-Eyed Archivist**  
- **Rank**: Outer Disciple (Records Division)  
- **Specialty**: Void Ink Script & Spatial Seal Arrays  
- **Personality**: Nervous, reclusive, speaks in short bursts.  
- **Notable Trait**: Can write temporary void glyphs that trap sound, light, or motion.

---

## 🧙‍♂️ Elders & Instructors

### 🌌 **Elder Mo Tianque — Starstep Elder**  
- **Rank**: Spatial Combat Instructor  
- **Specialty**: Star-Linked Sword Forms & Dimensional Compression  
- **Personality**: Stern, visionary, haunted by a failed breakthrough.  
- **Notable Trait**: Each of his sword strikes pulls a fragment of the night sky.  

---

### 🔹 **Elder Nian Fei — Keeper of the Echo Vault**  
- **Rank**: Guardian of the Sect’s Ancestral Vault  
- **Specialty**: Echo Dao & Chrono-Spatial Locking  
- **Personality**: Cryptic and ageless; speaks in reversed proverbs.  
- **Notable Trait**: Guards a sword that only appears at twilight.

---

## 🥀 Notable Defectors & Rogues

### 🕳 **Yan Lianhua — The Hollow Thorn**  
- **Former Rank**: Core Disciple (Missing)  
- **Specialty**: Spatial Thorns & Piercing Void Technique  
- **Personality**: Bitter, once kind; vanished during an inter-sect competition.  
- **Rumor**: May have joined the Mirror Abyss Sect to pursue forbidden edge arts.

---

### 🌓 **Qin Shao — The One-Step Traitor**  
- **Former Rank**: Inner Disciple  
- **Specialty**: Void Step & Betrayer’s Lunge  
- **Personality**: Arrogant, theatrical, blames the sect for his brother’s exile.  
- **Notable Trait**: Can shift behind you with a smirk before his blade draws blood.

---

## 🌀 Final Thought

The Hollow Edge Sect does not nurture ordinary swordsmen—it cultivates those who speak the **language of silence and shadow**, of edges that never touch yet still divide.

> “One fold of space, one life undone.”
