# 📖 Forbidden Scriptures (禁经)

> *"These books do not speak truths—they undo them."*  
> —Inscription found on a scorched vault wall in the Library of Silent Gold

---

## 🩸 What Are Forbidden Scriptures?

**Forbidden Scriptures** are rare, cursed, or erased tomes that contain knowledge deemed too dangerous, heretical, or corrosive for the mortal or immortal mind.

Some originate from the **Eight Desolates**, others from **the Immortal Abyss**, the corpses of fallen realms, or even fractured dreams of Dao Monarchs. These scriptures often rewrite the understanding of cultivation—or destroy it entirely.

They are not simply books; they are **phenomena**, each with its own will, memory, and laws.

---

## 🕯️ Identifying Features

| Feature                | Description                                                       |
|------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Appearance             | Warped scrolls, bone-inked tomes, blood-crusted slates, ash-mirrors |
| Linguistic Effect      | Cannot be translated—must be directly *absorbed* or *endured*     |
| Script Behavior        | Characters bleed, move, scream, or vanish if unread correctly     |
| Reader Reaction        | Madness, enlightenment, mutation, or Dao Fracture                 |
| Shelf-Life             | May self-destruct if purpose fulfilled—or become parasitic        |

---

## ⚔️ Known Examples

### 📚 Desolation Codex (荒经)
> A fragmented manual from the Ashen Veil containing the foundational principles of Desolate Immortality and Baptisms.

### 📜 Abyssal Sutra of Stillborn Stars (寂星经)
> Rewritten in starlight from a dead universe, it contains no techniques—only failed cultivations and their echoes.

### 📘 The Null Lotus Scroll
> Reading it erases your previous cultivation techniques, leaving only instinctual Desolate adaptations.

### 🔮 Fleshbound Mandala
> Scripture etched into living flesh—recited through pain. Each reading alters reality around the user for a brief moment.

## 🛑 Forbidden for a Reason

Even possessing one of these scriptures marks a cultivator for destruction by orthodox sects, Silent Lords, or the Heaven-Enforcing Legion.

Those who read them often become vectors for Desolation themselves.

> *"Knowledge cannot be undone, but the knower can."*  
> —Last words of the Immortal Archivist
