Excellent question. In a Xianxia world, hierarchy is everything—it dictates power, privilege, and the path of cultivation. Here is the detailed hierarchy of the Frigid School, from the frozen peak of power down to the hopeful aspirants below.

The Frozen Peak: Leadership & Ancestral Power

· The Grand Master (Bing Huang / 冰皇 - "Ice Sovereign")
  · The Absolute Zero. The living heart of the school's deepest secrets. This figure is less a person and more a force of nature, often in secluded meditation to suppress their own world-freezing power or to comprehend a deeper Law of Frost. Their word is final, absolute, and cold as permafrost.
· The Frost Council (Han Yi Hui / 寒议會 - "Cold Deliberation Assembly")
  · The Governing Glacier. Composed of 3 to 5 Supreme Elders (Zhi Zun Zhanglao / 至尊長老), each a millennia-old monster who has mastered a branch of the school's ultimate arts (e.g., the "Eternal Sleep Seal," the "Glacial Phantom Step," the "Heart-Freezing Scripture"). They make strategic decisions, guard forbidden vaults, and only emerge during existential crises.

The Pillars of Ice: Administration & Instruction

· The Sect Master (Men Zhu / 門主) or School Lord (Xue Gong / 雪宮之主)
  · The Active Glacier. The public face and operational ruler. Manages the sect's resources, diplomacy, and grand ceremonies. A powerhouse in their own right (often at the peak of the Nascent Soul or higher realm), executing the will of the Grand Master and Frost Council.
· The Hall Masters (Tang Zhu / 堂主)
  · The Specialized Peaks. Each leads a crucial hall with its own focus, territory, and resources within the school's icy domain:
    · Silent Frost Hall (Chen Shuang Tang / 沉霜堂): Internal law enforcement, discipline, and defense.
    · Frozen Alchemy Hall (Bing Dan Tang / 冰丹堂): Creation of ice-attributed pills, elixirs, and poisons.
    · Jade Refinement Hall (Yu Lian Tang / 玉煉堂): Crafting of ice-attribute spiritual tools, weapons, and artifacts.
    · Archives of the Frozen Echo (Bing Yin Cang Ge / 冰音藏閣): Custodians of scriptures, techniques, and ancient histories.
    · Outer Affairs Hall (Wai Shi Tang / 外事堂): Manages trade, mortal world contacts, and sect missions.
· Elders (Zhanglao / 長老)
  · The Venerable Ice. Senior experts who serve as advisors to Hall Masters, specialized instructors for ultimate techniques, or guardians of important spiritual sites (like the "Never-Thawing Spring" or the "Cave of Howling Wind").

The Core Frost: The Main Force of the Sect

· Core Disciples (Heixin Dizi / 核心弟子)
  · The Sharpest Icicles. The elite, handpicked successors. Each is a genius personally taken as a direct disciple by a Hall Master, Elder, or even the Sect Master. They learn profound techniques, have access to the best resources, and represent the school's future leadership. They often wear the reversible blue/white robes with cyan accents.
· Inner Disciples (Neimen Dizi / 內門弟子)
  · The Forming Glacier. The true foundation of the school. They have proven their talent and loyalty, dwelling in the inner sect's spiritual caves. They receive systematic instruction from Elders and senior Core Disciples, undertake dangerous missions, and strive to break through to higher realms. They wear the distinct Frost-White and Glacier Blue uniforms.

The Outer Rime: The Foundation & Prospects

· Outer Disciples (Waimen Dizi / 外門弟子)
  · The Packed Snow. The largest group. They live in the outer compounds, performing chores, managing sect industries, and undergoing basic training. Their life is one of harsh competition for limited resources, hoping to shine in the annual "Frost Ascension Tournament" to earn promotion to Inner Disciple. They wear the practical Moon-Pale Grey and blue sash.
· Discipline Attendants (Zhi Shi / 執事)
  · The Useful Frost. A unique class often composed of older Outer Disciples with stalled cultivation or talented mortals. They are the administrators, stewards, and managers who keep the sect's daily operations running—managing supply lines, kitchens, mortal servants, and logistics. Not true disciples, but respected within the system.
· Aspirants & Trial Disciples (Shoutu Dizi / 受徒弟子)
  · The Fresh Snow. Newly-recruited children or youths undergoing the brutal initial trials. They must withstand the cold, demonstrate mental fortitude, and show a sliver of spiritual root affinity for ice/water attributes. Many are sent away within the first year. They wear simple, unadorned grey coats.

Unique Positions:

· Frost Blade Enforcers (Shuang Ren Zhixing / 霜刃執刑): An elite force drawn from top Inner and Core Disciples. They are the sect's claws, executing high-stakes missions, hunting traitors, and acting as the vanguard in conflicts. They are feared even by other disciples.
· The Secluded Ice-Maidens/Guardians (Biyu Shouhu / 閉玉守護): A mysterious group, often former Core Disciples who have taken a vow of eternal silence and guard the most ancient, forbidden places of the sect, such as the sealed "Tomb of the First Frost". They are rarely seen, moving like ghosts.

Cultivation Realm Correlation (Typical Xianxia Framework):

· Aspirants/Outer Disciples: Qi Condensation
· Inner Disciples: Foundation Establishment
· Core Disciples: Golden Core
· Elders/Hall Masters: Nascent Soul
· Supreme Elders/Sect Master: Soul Transformation / Void Refinement
· The Grand Master: Tribulation Passage / Dawn of the Great Ice Dao

This structure creates a clear, competitive, and immersive ladder of power, where one's rank is visibly reflected in the uniform and dictates every aspect of life in the frigid, beautiful, and merciless world of the sect.
