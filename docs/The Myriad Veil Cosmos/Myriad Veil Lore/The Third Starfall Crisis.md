# 🌠 The Third Starfall Crisis (第三次陨星大劫 - Dì Sān Cì Yǔn Xīng Dà Jié)

> *"Three stars burned red, but only one fell. The others shattered—split by a blade that should not have existed."*  
> — From the celestial records of the Astral Obelisk Observatory

---

## 📅 Era
- **Date:** Late **4th Epoch**, just prior to the rise of the **Void-Severing Sword Sect**
- **Cycle:** Known as the *Epoch of Trembling Veils*

---

## 🌌 Summary
The **Third Starfall Crisis** refers to a cosmic calamity in which:
- **Three rogue stars**, corrupted by a primordial **Heaven-Eating Wyrm**, broke free from celestial bonds.
- Their trajectory threatened to **erase three major cultivation realms**:  
  - **Mirrorwind Heaven**  
  - **Yunxu Domain**  
  - **Outer Ring of the Jade Casket World**

Most great sects fell into chaos. Immortals debated evacuation, but it was too late. As the red stars descended—

### 💠 A single slash divided the sky.

---

## ⚔️ Intervention
- A **crimson sword arc** sliced one of the falling stars in half, causing its remnants to dissolve into astral vapor.
- The other two stars vanished without impact—**folded into nothingness** mid-descent.
- No one saw the wielder.  
  - Only traces of **spatial distortion** and **resonant Sword Dao tremors** were detected.

> *"The blade passed. No trace. But the sky refused to close for seven days."*  
> — Witness Record, Grand Oracle Lian Mingzhi

---

## 🧠 Historical Impact
- Sparked panic about **rogue celestial phenomena** and **primordial entities**.
- Marked the first appearance of a **Sword Dao intervention** on a cosmic scale.
- Inspired dozens of sects to seek the **"Silent Sword Cultivation Path."**

---

## 🧭 Aftermath
- Creation of the **Starshatter Accord**: pact among top celestial sects to monitor spatial anomalies.
- Rise of **Mirrorwatching School**, founded by sole witness **Li Feiyan**.
- The phrase *"It only takes one sword"* became synonymous with divine intervention.

---

## 🕯️ Legacy
The Third Starfall Crisis remains one of the greatest unsolved mysteries in the Myriad Veil Cosmos, a moment where:
- **Dao met catastrophe**—
- **and cleaved it apart.**

> *"Some blades are drawn not to fight fate... but to stop it from ever arriving."*  
> — Fragment attributed to the Hollow Edge Sect
