- **Karmic Backlash**: Altering time disrupts the Myriad Veil Cosmos’s balance, attracting the wrath of Heavenly Tribulations or cosmic entities.
- **Spiritual Strain**: Time-based techniques consume vast amounts of spiritual energy, risking exhaustion or cultivation deviation.
- **Paradox Vulnerability**: Excessive meddling with time can create paradoxes, potentially erasing the cultivator from existence.

## Cultural Significance
In the *Myriad Veil Cosmos*, the Time Dao is both revered and feared. Sects dedicated to its study, such as the **Order of the Eternal Current**, are secretive and selective, guarding their techniques fiercely. Time cultivators are often sought as advisors or oracles, yet many sects distrust them, wary of their ability to alter fate.
