# ❓ Why Don’t the Ascendants of the Nine Pillars Destroy the Eight Desolates or the Immortal Abyss?

> “Power is not absence of consequence. The higher one ascends, the heavier the chains.”

## 🌌 Context

The **Ascendants of the Nine Pillars** are beings who have harmonized with one or more of the [[Nine Pillars of Creation]], granting them power that eclipses **True Immortals**, **Desolate Immortals**, and **Abyssal Immortals**. So why don’t they simply **erase** the **Eight Desolates** or **collapse** the **Immortal Abyss**?

---

## ⚖️ Cosmic Inertia & Foundational Balance

**The Eight Desolates** and **Immortal Abyss** are not just “enemy realms” — they are **fundamental structures** of the multiversal framework.

- 🔗 **Desolates = Decay of the Dao**  
  Their existence allows **entropy**, **choice**, and **evolution** in the Dao.

- 🌑 **Abyss = Reversal of the Dao**  
  The Abyss reflects what the Dao *rejects*, and paradoxically strengthens the Dao by contrast.

> Destroying either would unravel the **tension** that makes cultivation, karma, and Samsara *possible*.

---

## 🧬 The Chain of Cause & Reversion

The higher one ascends, the more bound they are by **Principle Reversion** — a metaphysical law stating:

> “Any act which destabilizes foundational planes will reverse upon its initiator.”

If an Ascendant tried to **destroy** a foundational plane:
- The **Pillar they embody would begin to collapse**
- They may become **anti-Pillars** — inverted monstrosities, often consumed or sealed

---

## ☁️ The Will of the Pillars

Each Pillar has a **semi-sentient will**, akin to that of the Heavenly Dao. An Ascendant is not its master, but its **vessel**.

Attempts to use this power selfishly or destabilize reality would:
- Be **rejected by the Pillar**
- Result in **fragmentation of the Ascendant's soul**, known as a *Shatterfall*
- Risk the rise of a new **Dao Collapse**

---

## 🧲 Forbidden Synchrony

There is a delicate **triadic balance**:

| Realm | Role | Counterbalance |
|-------|------|----------------|
| **Nine Heavens** | Order, Samsara, Ascendancy | Immortal Abyss |
| **Immortal Abyss** | Reversal, Corruption, Rupture | Eight Desolates |
| **Eight Desolates** | Chaos, Ruin, Anti-Dao | Nine Heavens |

Disrupting one risks:
- Collapse of **Samsara**
- **Overgrowth** of Karma or Desolate Essence
- Awakening of **Pre-Dao Entities**, like the [[First Heaven Ascendants]]

---

## 🧩 Secret Reason (Hidden Lore)

> ✨ Some suspect the Ascendants **already tried**.  
> One theory posits the **Grave Star Descent** and the creation of the [[Oblivion Maw]] were *side effects* of a failed attempt to destroy the Desolates.

- That Ascendant is said to now **slumber within a reversed Pillar**, only partially real.
- Known only in whispers as *The Unmade Lawbearer*.

---

## 🧘 Why They Don’t Intervene

- 🧿 **Non-Interference Vows**: Many Ascendants swore ancient pacts during the rebinding of Samsara.
- 🌀 **Dimensional Anchoring**: Ascendants can't freely move outside Pillar-aligned zones without unraveling.
- 🗝️ **Their Power is Meant for Restoration**, not annihilation. To destroy is to risk repeating the [[Dao Collapse]].
