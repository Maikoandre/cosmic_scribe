# ☄️ Path of Body Cultivation
> In Qi cultivation, one pursued the Dao through *one*,  
> but in Body Cultivation one pursued the Dao through *all*.  
> Whether it be one or all, they all returned to unity and became the **Grand Dao**.

# ☄️ Physique Classifications
There are **three categories** of physiques:
1. **Innate Physiques** – Born with  
   > e.g., *Broken Fate Physique* of the Lost Immortal  
2. **Acquired Physiques** – Gained after cultivation  
   > e.g., *Xiantian Physique* after Body Tempering Realm  
3. **Unique Physiques** – Cannot be classified  
   > e.g., *Garden of Karma*

# ☄️ Voids
- **Lesser Void** – Parallel to real world; unique to each world  
- **Great Void** – Contains minor worlds and spatial dimensions  
- **Greater Void** – Contains multiple worlds in a realm  
- **Grand Void** – Separates major realms; contains all lower layers

> Each higher layer in the void contains multiple of the layer below it.

# ☄️ Types of Qi
There are **twelve main elemental Qis** classified into three tiers:

### Mortal Elements:
- Fire, Wood, Earth, Metal, Water

### Heavenly Elements:
- Wind, Lightning, Light, Darkness

### Primordial Elements:
- Space, Time, Chaos

> All other types are **combinations or derivations**.

### Qi Qualities:
- **Spirit Qi**  
- **Immortal Qi**  
- **True Qi**  
- **Vital Energy / Vital Essence**  
- **Immortal Essence / Elemental Essence**  
- **Spirit Sense / Immortal Sense**

> Qi may be infused with elements (e.g. Fire Spirit Qi), or become a **derivative Qi** (e.g. Sword Qi, Saber Qi).  
> True Qi comes from directly comprehending a Path or Dao.

> *"Every Path has its own True Qi. Gaining even one is enough to stand at the top of the world."*

# ☄️ Origin of the Universe
1. **Wuji** — The Infinite, the source of all  
2. **Taiji** — The Supreme  
3. **Liangyi** — Duality: Yin and Yang

### Primordial Elements:
- **Chaos**, **Time**, and **Space**

> Chaos existed in stillness, until *Time* began to flow.  
> As *Time* moved, *Chaos* expanded within *Space*, eventually exploding — becoming **Wuji**, from which all creation came.
