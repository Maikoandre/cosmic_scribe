### Cultivation System Overview

This cultivation system is called the **Celestial Meridian Path**, emphasizing the flow and refinement of spiritual energy within the cultivator.

***

### Cultivation Levels and Stages

1. **Mortal Stage**
   - Ordinary humans with no cultivation ability.
   - Physical and spiritual potential are latent.

2. **Qi Refining Realm**
   - **Foundation Establishment**: Absorb and condense natural Qi into the dantian.
   - **Core Formation**: Shape Qi into a stable energy core.
   - **Qi Consolidation**: Strengthen and refine the Qi core.

3. **Core Formation Realm**
   - **Spirit Core Generation**: Spiritual energy matures into a visible core.
   - **Core Ascendancy**: Core breaks through limits, gaining new powers.

4. **Nascent Soul Realm**
   - **Soul Nascent Birth**: Physical body and soul synchronize creating a Nascent Soul.
   - **Soul Refinement**: Passion, will, and spiritual strength are strengthened.

5. **Spirit Ascension Realm**
   - **Spirit Integration**: Merge with spiritual energy from the world.
   - **True Spirit Manifestation**: Summon Spirit Body or Guardian.

6. **Immortal Transformation Realm**
   - **Earthly Immortal**: Body transcends mortal limits.
   - **Heavenly Immortal**: Gain control over celestial forces.
   - **Void Immortal**: Break worldly bounds, entering heavenly dominions.

7. **Celestial Dao Realm**
   - **Dao Comprehension**: Understand universal laws.
   - **Dao Mastery**: Manipulate Dao energy, granting near-divine power.
   - **Great Dao Fusion**: Fuse with Dao itself, transcending mortality.

***

### Core Concepts

- **Qi (Spiritual Energy):** The energy from nature and the universe, absorbed through breathing, meditation, and special techniques.
- **Meridians:** Channels inside the body that transport Qi. Opening and clearing meridians is essential.
- **Dantian:** The energy storage core inside the body.
- **Spiritual Roots:** The innate ability to cultivate. Differ in quality, affecting cultivation speed and potential.

***

### Resources and Materials

- **Heavenly Herbs:** Plants with natural Qi to assist cultivation.
- **Spirit Stones:** Crystals that store and emit spiritual energy.
- **Sacred Artifacts:** Rare tools that enhance cultivation, protect, or assist in battle.
- **Celestial Pills:** Alchemical creations that boost different attributes (Qi, body, soul).

***

### Cultivation Techniques

- **Breath Cultivation:** Basic Qi absorption through breath control.
- **Soul Meditation:** Focus on strengthening and refining the Nascent Soul.
- **Meridian Guidance:** Martial arts style that opens meridians and circulates Qi.
- **Formations and Arrays:** Use spiritual formations to amplify cultivation or defense.
- **Spirit Summoning:** Call forth spirit animals or guardians as companions.

***

### Unique Traits

- **Qi Compatibility Spectrum:** Cultivators can specialize and become exceptionally powerful by aligning their Qi with different elements (Fire Qi, Water Qi, Void Qi).
- **Karmic Bonding:** A cultivator's destiny affects their cultivation, allowing for karmic power boosts or penalties.
- **Trial of Heaven:** High-level cultivators face “Heavenly Trials” that test their strength, will, and understanding before advancing.
