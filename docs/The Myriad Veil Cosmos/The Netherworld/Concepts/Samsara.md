# ♻️ Samsara (轮回 • Lúnhuí)

> *“All must return. All must begin again.”*

---

## 🌌 Overview

**Samsara**, also known as the **Cycle of Rebirth**, is the cosmic mechanism by which all sentient beings are continually reborn into new lives based on the karmic weight of their actions. It binds mortals, spirits, and even lesser immortals in an endless cycle of birth, death, and reincarnation.

It is **older than the Nine Heavens**, and yet **younger than the Dao Collapse**. Formed in the aftermath of the primordial fragmentation of the Dao, Samsara is not a law—it is a cage.

---

## 🔁 The Six Paths of Rebirth (六道 • Liù Dào)

Samsara is governed by the **Six Paths**, each a realm of existence reflecting karmic consequence:

| Path                    | Description                                                                 |
|-------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Heavenly Path (天道)**     | Beings of great virtue and karma; live in celestial bliss but are ignorant of suffering. |
| **Human Path (人道)**        | The mortal realm—balanced between suffering and joy; best path for cultivation.         |
| **Asura Path (修罗道)**      | Beings of conflict and wrath; powerful but consumed by jealousy and battle.             |
| **Beast Path (畜生道)**      | Realm of instinct and ignorance. Beings are ruled by base desire and fear.              |
| **Hungry Ghost Path (饿鬼道)**| Beings consumed by endless hunger, regret, or obsession.                                |
| **Hell Path (地狱道)**       | Realm of pure suffering. Reserved for those of grievous sin.                              |

---

## 🌀 The Wheel of Samsara (轮回之轮)

In the spirit world, Samsara is symbolized by the **Wheel of Reincarnation**—a divine construct said to be forged from the bones of a Dao Ancestor who tried to escape cause and effect.

- **Core**: Holds the karmic imprint of every soul.
- **Rim**: Spins based on merit (功德 - Gōngdé) and sin (罪孽 - Zuìniè).
- **Spokes**: Connect the Six Paths and determine one’s rebirth.

---

## ⚖️ Karma & Merit

| Concept     | Description                                                                 |
|-------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Karma (因果)**   | The cosmic law of cause and effect. Every action, thought, and intention plants a seed. |
| **Merit (功德)**   | Spiritual virtue accumulated through righteous acts, enlightenment, or sacrifice. |

Together, they govern one's destiny within Samsara.

---

## 🧘‍♂️ Cultivation & Samsara

For most beings, Samsara is inescapable. But cultivators seek to **break free** or **manipulate the cycle**:

- **Soul Anchoring**: Preserve identity through cycles of death.
- **Reincarnation Control**: High-level cultivators may choose where or how they reincarnate.
- **Samsaric Dao Comprehension**: Cultivators who master this Dao can trap enemies in endless death-rebirth loops.
- **True Immortality**: The complete severance of Samsaric bondage.

---

## ☯️ Entities of Samsara

- **Wheelkeeper (轮主)**: A mythical judge said to oversee reincarnations.
- **Soul Ferrymen (引魂使)**: Entities who guide souls into the correct path of rebirth.
- **Cycle Breakers**: Rare beings or cultivators who transcend Samsara and no longer reincarnate.

---

## 🧿 Anomalies & Dangers

| Anomaly                      | Description                                                                 |
|------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Reincarnation Drift**      | A soul loses memory and karma, reborn out of alignment.                     |
| **Karmic Fragmentation**     | A soul is split across multiple paths—an unstable and dangerous fate.       |
| **False Samsara Realms**     | Imitations of reincarnation cycles created by heretical sects or evil gods. |

---

## 🕳️ In the Eight Desolates

In the [[Eight Desolates]], Samsara has **no reach**. Souls that perish in places like the [[Oblivion Maw]], [[Frozen Karma Wastes]], or [[Crimson Hollow]] become **lost echoes**, drifting outside the karmic system. 

This makes the Desolates both a **threat** and a **path to freedom** for those who seek to escape Heaven’s order.
