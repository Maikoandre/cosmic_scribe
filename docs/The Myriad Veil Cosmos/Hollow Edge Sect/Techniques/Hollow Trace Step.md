# 🪶 Hollow Trace Step (空迹步)

> *“A step unseen, a presence unfelt, a trace unleft.”*  
> — Described in the sealed Mirror Codex of the Hollow Edge Sect

---

## 📖 Overview

- **Technique Name**: Hollow Trace Step (空迹步)  
- **Art Type**: Advanced Spatial Movement / Concealment Technique  
- **Dao Roots**: Spatial Dao • Illusion Dao • Sword Dao (auxiliary)  
- **Parent Art**: Folded Presence Art (折隐术)  
- **Origin**: Hollow Edge Sect — developed within the Folded Mirror Gardens  
- **Mastery Tier**: Forbidden for Outer Disciples; restricted to elite Inner Disciples, Core Disciples, and Silent Edge operatives

---

## ✨ Description

**Hollow Trace Step** is a refinement of the foundational **Folded Presence Art**, which allows a cultivator to subtly warp their presence across spatial folds to become undetectable to normal spiritual senses. 

Where Folded Presence *obscures*, **Hollow Trace Step erases**—not only concealing location and qi signature, but **removing the causality** of one's step from the observable timeline.

Practitioners seem to “walk through emptiness,” leaving no footprints, sound, or trace in spirit or memory. The world registers only the *absence* where they should have been.

---

## 🔹 Function & Application

### 🪞 Primary Effects:
- **Step Between Recognition**: The user’s movement is not perceived, even by those watching directly.  
- **Spatial Trace Nullification**: Removes spiritual imprints, making detection via formations or fate techniques nearly impossible.  
- **Causal Severing**: In advanced forms, the technique severs the *record* of one’s step from planar memory.  

### 🧊 Advanced Applications:
- **Phantom Layer Shift**: Leaves a "mirror shell" behind that acts as a false presence.  
- **Echo Step Trap**: Creates a delayed reflection of the step, disorienting pursuit.  
- **Sword Fade Initiation** *(Silent Edge only)*: Allows activation of sword techniques mid-step while still erased.

---

## 🌀 Training Methodology

Training in Hollow Trace Step requires:

- At least **3-Fold Spatial Comprehension**  
- Meditative attunement inside the **Folded Mirror Gardens**  
- Isolation from karma-binding rituals for seven days  
- Completion of the “Mirror Self Disappearance Trial” — where the cultivator must walk through their own reflection and emerge without identity attachment

---

## ⚠️ Risks

> *“One step too far, and you return as no one.”*  
> — Elder Shen of the Hollow Edge Sect

- **Reflection Loss**: Losing resonance with one's mirrored self may lead to spiritual fragmentation.  
- **Dimensional Displacement**: Stepping through the wrong fold can send the user into a temporal echo or non-space.  
- **Dao Disassociation**: Overuse may erode presence so severely that one’s own Dao Weapon may fail to recognize its master.

## 🧪 Notable Practitioners

- **Lan Yiran**: Used Hollow Trace Step to eliminate a formation elder without tripping any defensive arrays.  
- **Mirror Five** of the Silent Edge: Known for stringing multiple Hollow Steps into the legendary “Silent Horizon Walk,” disappearing and reappearing behind multiple cultivators simultaneously.  
- **Elder Su Ruyan**: Developed the “Reverberation Loop” variant, using Hollow Trace Step in mirrored dimensions for remote strikes.

---

## 🔚 Summary

The **Hollow Trace Step** is a movement technique for those who no longer wish to be followed, sensed, or remembered. It is not merely speed or stealth—it is the **absence of consequence**. To master it is to erase the notion that you were ever there.
