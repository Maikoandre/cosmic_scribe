# 🌌 Folded Presence Art  
*“To be unseen, become unlocatable. To be unlocatable, do not exist where others look.”*

---

## 📜 Overview

- **Name**: Folded Presence Art  
- **Type**: Concealment / Spatial Qi Control  
- **Grade**: High Earth → Evolves to Low Heaven  
- **Sect**: Hollow Edge Sect (Core Inner Disciple Transmission)  
- **Dao Affinity**: Spatial Dao, Illusion Dao, Void Dao, Sword Intent  

---

## 📖 Description

The **Folded Presence Art** is a secret technique of the Hollow Edge Sect that allows cultivators to **retract their presence from the known world**. Unlike basic stealth or illusion techniques, this art works by folding one’s physical, spiritual, and qi presence into **adjacent layers of space**, effectively making the user imperceptible to others.

By aligning one's intent with the principles of **non-resonant existence**, the user becomes a **folded echo** in the world—unseen, unheard, and unfelt.

> "Instead of hiding your breath, cease to exhale into this layer of reality."

---

## 🌀 Effects

| Effect               | Description |
|----------------------|-------------|
| **Qi Nullification** | Suppresses qi to match ambient spiritual energy, rendering aura undetectable. |
| **Spatial Displacement** | Shifts user into a mirrored spatial fold, invisible to standard vision or divine sense. |
| **Soul Veil**        | Detaches spiritual imprint from local karmic threads, masking identity and intent. |
| **Sound Looping**    | Redirects noise generated by the body into recursive folds, creating auditory silence. |
| **Breath of Absence** *(Advanced)* | Mimics a non-cultivator presence entirely; can fool sect detection formations and beast senses. |

---

## 🧘 Cultivation Requirements

- Mastery of the **3rd Layer of the Spatial Dao** — *“Folding, Not Moving.”*
- 49 days of silent meditation in a **Folded Mirror Garden** under vow of stillness.
- Sword Intent refined to the level of **cutting without motion**.

---

## ⚠️ Limitations

- Disrupted by intense emotions, killing intent, or battle qi.
- Can be faintly perceived by those with **Karma**, **Time**, or **Truth-seeing** Daos.
- Spatial ruptures or mirror backlash may cause **phase-lock**, temporarily freezing the user in place.

## 🔘 Advanced Variant

### **Hollow Trace Step**
Combines Folded Presence Art with Void Footwork techniques. The user may **shift through folded space without producing afterimages, footsteps, or memory trails**.  
At higher levels, enemies may be **unable to recall** that the cultivator ever stood in a given space.

> “Those who reach the Dao of the Hollow Edge need not hide. They simply fold themselves out of your reality.”
