# ⚔️ Battle of Silent Oblivion (寂灭之战)

> *“No record remains. No echoes persist. But something changed the Grave forever.”*  
> — Fragment 3, ⟪Desolation Codex⟫

---

## 🕳️ Event Summary

In the forgotten depths of the **Echoing Grave of the Dao**, a realm where broken Dao concepts drift like ash, a confrontation of incomprehensible magnitude once occurred:

A battle between a **Desolate Immortal**—a being who defied Heaven’s design—and an **Abyssal Immortal**, one who dissolved all identity within the Immortal Abyss.

Neither fought for dominance, belief, or survival.  
They clashed because both existed where **nothing else should**.

---

## 🌌 Location

**Echoing Grave of the Dao**  
> A desolate realm filled with failed paths of enlightenment and shattered Dao laws. Here, even truth bleeds.

- **Effects at the time of battle**:
  - Spatial structure collapsed into recursive paradox.
  - Conceptual gravity inverting every few seconds.
  - All observing constructs (even divine or artifact-based) **ceased functioning**.

---

## ⚔️ Combatants

### 🩸 The Desolate Immortal (无天孤影)

> “His body walked without shadow. His will burned without name.”

- Master of a forbidden Desolation Path.
- Bore the **Crown of the Void Epoch**, forged in the Witherwind Expanse.
- Known for the **Desolation Sutra of Unmaking**—a technique that erodes causality itself.

### 🕳️ The Abyssal Immortal (渊仙)

> “It did not move. It did not act. The world came undone regardless.”

- No name, no presence—identified only by anti-Qi signatures and reality corrosion patterns.
- Did not display techniques; its **existence alone reversed reality flow**.
- Eyes described in a single surviving line: “Abyss within abyss.”

## 🧩 Outcome

No victor was ever declared.

- The Desolate Immortal vanished.  
- The Abyssal Immortal remained seated where it last stood, unmoving, partially fused with Dao stone.
- The surrounding realm collapsed inward—forming what is now called the **Oblivion Meridian**, a zone of permanent paradox and unrememberable reality.

---

## 🧬 Implications

- Believed to be the origin of the **Dao Fracture Pulse**, which still echoes through certain desolate domains.
- Several sects claim fragments of the **Desolate Immortal’s battle will** remain hidden in the **Ashen Veil** and **Crimson Hollow**.
- Some heretical scholars argue the clash was not combat—but **a failed synthesis of opposing immortal truths**.
