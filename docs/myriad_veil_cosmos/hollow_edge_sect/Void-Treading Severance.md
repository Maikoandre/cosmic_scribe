# 🗡️ Void-Treading Severance (踏虚断劫)

> *“Where space folds, this blade walks. Where reality binds, it cuts free.”*

---

## 📜 Lore

**Void-Treading Severance** is a legendary **Forged Tribulation Dao Weapon** born in the **Folded Mirror Gardens** of the Hollow Edge Sect during a rare **Ninefold Spatial Heavenly Tribulation**.

Its original wielder, **Lan Yiran**, ascended beyond core formation after surviving the Folded Trial of Infinite Echoes, tempering his sword with tribulation lightning that had fractured through multiple dimensions. The result was a sentient blade attuned not just to space, but to the void **between realms**.

### 🔹 Traits

- **Dimensional Shear**: Slashes that bypass physical distance, cutting targets across foldspace.
- **Veil-Step Activation**: Teleports the user a short distance **between moments**, disrupting cause and effect.
- **Silent Rend**: Strikes without spatial vibration; the cut exists before it is perceived.

Legend says that the blade **remembers each spatial wound it inflicts**, subtly attuning itself to multidimensional combat patterns. Only those attuned to both **Sword Dao** and **Spatial Dao** can even unsheathe it.

## ⚔️ Combat Scene: "Folded Kill"

> *Location: The Star-Wound Canyon, 3rd Strata of the Mirror Sky Realms*

The wind howled between broken ridges of suspended stone, shimmering with fractured starlight. Dozens of assassins from the Crimson Veil Alliance encircled the young cultivator—**Lan Yiran**—their positions tightly woven in a dome of spatial bindings.

"Nowhere to run," one smirked, weaving a dimensional collapse sigil.

Lan Yiran exhaled once. The bindings flared—

And then **stilled**.

No sound. No motion.

Only a blur—a ripple where the light had not yet decided to follow.

As the sigil-keeper blinked, his midsection opened like a seam in cloth. Behind him, another veil-walker screamed as his formation talisman fell into **its own shadow**.

One by one, they fell.

Not as if struck—but as if **cut out of the scene** itself.

In the stillness that followed, Void-Treading Severance hummed low, its blade catching nothing—**not blood, not reflection, not air**—only the emptiness of a space that never was.

> *"Your formation stitched reality. Mine unpicked it."*

Lan Yiran vanished again—this time without even the courtesy of a ripple.
