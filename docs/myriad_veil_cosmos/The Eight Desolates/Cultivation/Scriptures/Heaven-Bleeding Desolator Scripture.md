# 🩸 Heaven-Bleeding Desolator Scripture (天溃荒神诀)

> **Immortal-Tier Desolation Body Cultivation Method**  
> *Forged in Ruin. Tempered by Silence. Fed by Cataclysm.*

---

## 📜 Origin

Carved in corpse-metal upon the calcified ribs of a fallen **Desolate Immortal**, impaled across the **Wailing Spires** of the **Third Desolate**. This scripture is not passed down—it is *endured*, drawn into the soul like rust into bone.

It does not elevate the practitioner.  
It *destroys* everything that is weak.

---

## 🕯️ Description

The **Heaven-Bleeding Desolator Scripture** is a heresy in the eyes of the Dao. Unlike orthodox body arts that harmonize the Three Treasures (Essence, Qi, and Spirit), this scripture *ruptures* them—reconstructing the body into a vessel of anti-life, capable of surviving the entropic storms of the **Eight Desolates**.

Rather than refining Qi into purity, it harnesses:
- **Withering Qi** (枯气 – *Kū Qì*)
- **Desolate Essence** (荒髓 – *Huāng Suǐ*)

These dead energies are scavenged from ruined worlds, abyssal fiend-beasts, and fragmented Dao remnants. The result is not a giant or golden god—but a being of catastrophic flesh, a walking echo of apocalyptic ruin.

---

## 🔥 Stages of Cultivation

1. **Ash-Marrow Reconstitution** (灰髓蜕生)  
   Burn the bone marrow with Desolate Ash from a ruined world. After nine nights of agony, the body bleeds ash instead of blood. This grants immunity to spirit-based corrosion and shatters spiritual techniques on contact.

2. **Vein of Silence Weaving** (寂脉重织)  
   Replace all nerves and tendons with Withering Threads from the Void Crust. Movement becomes jagged, sudden, and *immune to spatial disruptions or teleportation*.

3. **Bone-Wreck Citadel** (残骨铸城)  
   Bones are reforged with crushed Oblivion Stone, creating indestructible frameworks that rebound all force with brutal intensity. Each movement resonates with ruin.

4. **Desolate Heart Cultivation** (荒心初凝)  
   The heart condenses into a black star. It absorbs pain, consumes toxins, and replaces emotion with ceaseless vitality. No fatigue. No fear. No mercy.

5. **Heaven-Dissolving Avatar** (溃天化身)  
   Manifest an avatar of Entropic Flesh. Misshapen and primal, it resists destruction and can scream apart reality, unraveling Dao formations and reducing spatial domains to *voidsand*.

**Type:** Sonic / Spiritual Pressure  
**Effect:** Dao Disruption & Silence

- Shatters spirit-based barriers and techniques.

## 🦶 Step of World-Withering (踏碎寰宇)

**Type:** Desolate Body / Terrain Affliction  
**Effect:** Qi Depletion & Environment Corruption

A single stomp that drains the vitality of the land. Turns Qi rivers into sludge, extinguishes pill auras, and renders talismans inert for miles.

- Corrupts spiritual veins and suppresses cultivation resources.
- Weakens alchemical, talismanic, and formation-based tools.
- Spreads a miasma that makes healing arts backfire.

**Type:** Ultimate / Cataclysmic Invocation  
**Effect:** Planetary Impact & Realm-Wide Corruption

Summons a dying celestial body—corrupted by Withered Qi and bound in chains of entropy—to descend upon the battlefield. As it falls, the skies wail and spiritual laws shudder.

- Causes continent-scale destruction upon impact.
- Imbues the terrain with permanent anti-life essence.
- Temporarily suppresses Immortal Daos and cuts off access to higher realms in the vicinity.

> "Where it lands, no seed grows, no chant echoes, and no Dao flows."

**Type:** Revival / Desolate Rebirth

- Loses a portion of sanity or karma each time used.
- Can only be triggered once every major realm.

## ☠️ Cultivation Requirements

- Must submerge in the **Entropy Veins** of a dead realm.  
- Survive a **Desolation Storm** *bare-bodied*.  
- Consume either a **Shattered Dao Core** or a **Desolate Beast Heart**.  
- Must renounce all hope of enlightenment. Only through acceptance of ruin can one endure the scripture’s path.

## ❗ Side Effects & Curses

- **Desolation Feedback**: Each breakthrough erodes a fragment of the mind. Without soul-anchoring artifacts, madness is inevitable.
- **Silence Curse**: The voice fades with each stage. By the final transformation, speech is impossible—only soul-pressure or Desolate Aura can communicate intent.
- **Karmic Reversal**: All accumulated good karma inverts. The Heavens brand the cultivator a *virus*, and future tribulations increase in both intensity and frequency.
