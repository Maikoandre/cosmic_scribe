# 🩸 Crimson Hollow (血狱空 • Xuèyù Kōng)

> *"War without end. Corpses without rot. Screams that echo across eternity."*

---

## 🌌 Overview

The **Crimson Hollow** is the second dominion among the [[Eight Desolates]], a blood-drenched expanse of eternal conflict where death holds no dominion, and the battlefield never sleeps.

Here, the echoes of endless war persist long after the warriors have fallen—if they ever truly did. Swords drift through the air still stained with immortal ichor. War banners flutter in winds that carry screams from a thousand ages. No corpse decays, yet no soul can escape.

It is a realm where **martial will**, **slaughter**, and **wrath** have become the very air itself.

---

## ⚔️ Core Nature

- **Perpetual Battlefields**: Plains, mountains, and skies are littered with the weapons and remains of wars lost to time. Each location replays its own eternal conflict.
- **Unrotting Dead**: Corpses remain intact, their blood always fresh, often rising to fight once more at the whisper of war.
- **Soul Entanglement**: Slain spirits do not pass on, trapped in endless cycles of conflict, sometimes possessing the living or reanimating their former bodies.
- **Living Weapons**: Some blades and spears gain sentience through millennia of bloodshed, whispering battle hymns or mad commands to anyone who grasps them.

## 🧬 Cultivation Relevance

- **Slaughter Dao Comprehension (杀道悟法)**: Many warriors have refined powerful martial Daos through prolonged exposure to the Hollow’s ambient carnage.
- **Battle Will Tempering**: Cultivators who survive the Hollow often develop unshakable willpower and battle instincts that transcend logic or fear.
- **Blood Echo Forging**: Rare techniques allow cultivators to reforge weapons from ancient blood resonances, creating tools that carry entire wars within them.
- **Crimson Core Physique**: A rare body type awakened through constant death-duels in the Hollow, granting resilience, rage-enhanced strength, and necrotic Qi manipulation.

---

## 📜 Legends & Lore

> *"No matter the victor, the Crimson Hollow will not rest. It was never a war—it is war itself."*

- It is believed that the Hollow was once the site of a battle between **Thronefallen war gods**, whose final clash tore open a scar in the fabric of the Dao.
- Some say the **first sword ever forged** still floats here, eternally dueling itself.
- Others believe the Hollow is secretly feeding on combat itself, growing stronger as new warriors fall and old battles replay.
