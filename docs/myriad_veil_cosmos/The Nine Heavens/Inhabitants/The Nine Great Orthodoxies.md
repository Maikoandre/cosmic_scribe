# 🏯 The Nine Great Orthodoxies (九大正统 - Jiǔ Dà Zhèngtǒng)

> *"Nine pillars uphold the heavens—each one carved from a truth older than stars."*  
> — Proverb recorded in the Astral Canon of Ages

---

## 📜 Overview

The **Nine Great Orthodoxies** are the most prestigious and time-honored sects within the *Myriad Veil Cosmos*. Recognized by both mortals and immortals, these sects are entrusted with guarding the balance of cultivation, safeguarding the righteous path, and preserving ancient legacies that predate many worlds.

These sects:
- Possess lineage tracing back to **immortal founders or cosmic events**.
- Rule over **unique realms or sealed domains**.
- Hold mastery over a **specific Dao or elemental law**.
- Often serve as arbiters during realm-wide conflicts.

---

## 🗺️ The Nine Great Orthodoxies

### 1. 🗡️ Void-Severing Sword Sect (斩空剑宗 - Zhǎn Kōng Jiànzōng)
- **Specialty:** Space-cutting Sword Dao, Dimensional Blades  
- **Motto:** *"A single cut divides heaven and earth."*  
- **Domain:** Cracked Sky Expanse  
- **Rivalry:** Bloodthirst Demon Hall  
- **Legacy Art:** Voidrend Blade Technique

### 2. 🌌 Star-Forged Palace (星铸宫 - Xīng Zhù Gōng)
- **Specialty:** Star Dao, Celestial Weapon Crafting  
- **Motto:** *"What is forged in stars cannot break."*  
- **Domain:** Astral Crucible  
- **Notes:** Known for crafting Dao-tier weapons during cosmic convergences.

### 3. 🧿 Ethereal Dream Hall (虚梦殿 - Xū Mèng Diàn)
- **Specialty:** Dream Dao, Illusions, Soul Arts  
- **Motto:** *"In dreams, we awaken deeper truths."*  
- **Domain:** Boundless Reverie Lake  
- **Notes:** Some of their techniques are considered borderline heretical.

### 4. 🪷 Azure Lotus Pavilion (青莲阁 - Qīng Lián Gé)
- **Specialty:** Dao of Purity, Spiritual Enlightenment  
- **Motto:** *"In stillness, the Dao blooms."*  
- **Domain:** Floating Lotus Realm  
- **Strength:** Renowned for sage-like cultivators and grand alchemists.

### 5. 🔥 Scarlet Yang Temple (赤阳神殿 - Chì Yáng Shéndiàn)
- **Specialty:** Flame Dao, Solar Essence, Martial Combat  
- **Motto:** *"Burn brighter than fate."*  
- **Domain:** Flameheart Plateau  
- **Trait:** Trains Sacred Yang Physique cultivators.

### 6. 🕊️ Celestial Crane Sect (天鹤宗 - Tiān Hè Zōng)
- **Specialty:** Wind Dao, Lightfoot Arts, Sky Movement  
- **Motto:** *"Let your feet never touch the dust of the world."*  
- **Domain:** Skyfeather Range  
- **Notes:** Their speed techniques are unparalleled in the lower heavens.

### 7. ⛩️ Divine Echo Monastery (神回寺 - Shén Huí Sì)
- **Specialty:** Sound Dao, Karma Manipulation, Harmony  
- **Motto:** *"Echo the heavens, silence the void."*  
- **Domain:** Sonorous Cloud Reaches  
- **Role:** Often mediators and judges in sect disputes.

### 8. 🌿 Verdant Immortal Hall (苍生仙宫 - Cāng Shēng Xiān Gōng)
- **Specialty:** Life Dao, Herbal Sovereignty, Vital Essence  
- **Motto:** *"To heal is to shape destiny."*  
- **Domain:** Immortal Grove of Ten Thousand Roots  
- **Contributions:** Created foundational medicinal arts used across sects.

### 9. 🌩️ Thunder Abyss Sect (雷渊宗 - Léi Yuān Zōng)
- **Specialty:** Lightning Dao, Heavenly Tribulation Harnessing  
- **Motto:** *"Ride the storm, become the storm."*  
- **Domain:** Abyssal Thunderfield  
- **Note:** Their cultivators often volunteer as tribulation guides.

---

## 🧭 Function & Authority

- **Guardians of Balance:** Ensure the righteous path persists amid rising heresies.
- **Gatekeepers of Heaven's Will:** Host realm-wide ascension rites and confer blessings.
- **Epochal Decision-Makers:** Ratify universal laws, sect accords, and cosmic armistices.

---

## 🔮 Rumors & Secrets

- Whispers speak of a **Tenth Orthodoxy**, either erased from history or sealed by the Heavenly Dao.
- Some sects are suspected of forming **secret pacts with Immortal Beasts** or **rogue Daos**.
- A hidden concord may exist to **limit True Immortal emergence** within a generation.
