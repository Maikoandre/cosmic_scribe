# 🌌 True Immortals of the Nine Heavens （九天真仙）

> "To step into the Nine Heavens is to bear the will of eternity. To become a True Immortal is to burn brighter than constellations—and fall harder than worlds."

---

## 🪐 Overview

The **True Immortals of the Nine Heavens** are transcendent cultivators who have pierced through mortal shackles and ascended beyond the Veils of Samsara. They are the paragons of orthodoxy, wielders of celestial authority, and guardians (or tyrants) of the Heavenly Order.

Their power begins **equal** to that of the feared **Desolate Immortals** of the Eight Desolates and the cursed **Abyssal Immortals** of the Immortal Abyss. But unlike their counterparts, True Immortals are nourished by Heavenly Qi and karmic merit rather than decay or void.

They are the enforcers of the Celestial Mandate, yet even they fear the forgotten truths buried in desolate ruins and abyssal depths.

---

## 🧬 Origins & Ascension

True Immortality is achieved through:
- **Passing the Immortal Ascension Tribulation** (飞升天劫)
- **Condensing Immortal Qi (仙气)** through pure Dao comprehension
- **Shattering the Heavenly Seal**, a karmic lock binding all mortals

The moment of ascension cleaves one’s fate from the mortal plane and etches their soul into the Celestial Record (天籍).

---

## ☯️ Characteristics

- **Immortal Body (不朽之体)**: Immune to decay, poison, disease. Can regenerate from annihilation given enough Immortal Qi.
- **Immortal Soul (不灭之神识)**: Endures tribulations of the soul, can persist outside the body indefinitely.
- **Dao Domain (道域)**: A metaphysical territory where the Immortal's laws supersede those of Heaven and Earth.
- **Flight Across Starfields**: Capable of traversing cosmic distances without aid.
- **Immortal Will (仙意)**: The ability to impose one’s Dao onto reality.

---

## 🏛️ Celestial Factions

Many True Immortals serve or rule within the great sects and divine courts of the Nine Heavens:

- **Celestial Sword Pavilion (天剑阁)**
- **Heavenly Mandate Court (天命殿)**
- **Jade Radiance Sect (玉辉宗)**
- **Thousand Lotus Heaven (千莲天界)**

These factions often clash in heavenly wars, their disputes shaking the realms below like divine tempests.

---

## ⚔️ Relationship to Other Immortals

- 🏴‍☠️ **Desolate Immortals**: Viewed as corruptions of the Dao. Some True Immortals hunt them, others secretly study them.
- 🕳️ **Abyssal Immortals**: Regarded with dread. Their rejection of the Celestial Mandate and defilement of karma makes them anathema.
- 🔥 **Transcendent Beings**: Rare True Immortals may ascend further, becoming Sages or Cosmic Overseers.

## ❗ Secrets & Heresies

- ⚠️ Not all True Immortals are loyal to Heaven.
- Some seek **Desolate Immortality**, abandoning karmic purity.
- Others have **fallen into Abyssal corruption**, wearing masks of light while cultivating void.

It is said even within the Jade Heavens, **true harmony is a lie**—and the Dao watches silently as the Immortals turn upon one another.

---

> 🗝️ In the end, the title "True Immortal" is not a promise of eternity. It is a burden—a crown of lightning balanced on the blade of karma.
