# 🌌 Heaven-Silencing Oblivion Scripture (寂天虚灭经)

> *“When the Void speaks, even the Heavens fall silent.”*  
> — Inscription found at the shattered altar of the Silent Sky Watchers

---

## 📜 Overview

The **Heaven-Silencing Oblivion Scripture** is the **supreme Void Dao technique**, a mythic cultivation scripture that does not merely defy the Heavenly Dao—it seeks to **unmake it**. Said to have been composed in the final moments before the **Dao Collapse**, this scripture holds **conceptual verses** that destabilize divine laws, unravel karma, and sever fate.

> Only those who have fully abandoned their name, fate, and reflection may attempt its recitation.

---

## ⚙️ Nature

- **Type**: Absolute Forbidden Technique  
- **Alignment**: Void Dao (虚空之道)  
- **Purpose**: *Erase the voice of the Heavenly Dao and nullify its will*  
- **Tier**: Transcendent-Void / Anti-Cosmic

---

## 📖 Effects

### 🔥 1. *Verse of Null Creation* (无始虚章)
> Cancels the concept of genesis in a localized zone — nothing can be born, summoned, or created while the verse echoes.

- Disables formation arrays, soul summoning, and rebirth.
- Blocks pill formation, beast birth, and reincarnation.

---

### ⚡ 2. *Stanza of Dao Erasure* (灭道篇)
> Severs cultivators from their path entirely. Techniques fail. Dao hearts fracture.

- Can reduce a Heaven-ranked cultivator to a mortal in moments.
- Extremely risky: has a 50% chance to sever user's own Dao as well.

---

### 🕳️ 3. *Chorus of Unhearing Heavens* (天聋合吟)
> The Heavenly Dao’s will is silenced, unable to issue divine retribution or fate correction.

- Celestial Tribulations cease to respond.
- Karmic backlash becomes inert—for a time.

---

### 🧠 4. *Codex of Self-Unmaking* (自湮圣典)
> User slowly erases themselves from the karmic web to gain full immunity to divine detection.

- Perfect stealth from fate-reading, Samsara tracking, and celestial decree.
- User risks ego-dissolution if the codex is not reversed properly.

---

## 🚨 Consequences

| Effect                         | Backlash Risk                            |
|-------------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| *Stanza of Dao Erasure*       | Complete loss of cultivation base        |
| *Codex of Self-Unmaking*      | Irrecoverable identity and soul erosion  |
| *Chorus of Unhearing Heavens* | Provokes latent retaliation by Heaven    |

- If used more than once, **Heaven may dispatch a True Immortal** to personally intervene.
- Some say that **Ascendants of the Nine Pillars** watch for echoes of this scripture across realms.

---

## 🕯️ Origins & Lore

- Believed to be compiled by the **First Nameless One**, a cultivator who existed before the Dao had voice.
- Fragments of the scripture exist in:
  - The **Oblivion Maw**
  - A sealed well in the **Fifth Heaven**
  - The thought-core of the **Severance Crowned**

> Few dare recite even a single verse. Fewer still remember what came after.
