# 🗡️🌀 Spatial Sword Dao  
*Blade Without Distance | The Edge That Bends Reality*

> “He did not swing faster than light—he simply erased the space between himself and his target.”

---

## 🌌 Overview  
The **Spatial Sword Dao** is a rare and transcendent fusion of two of the most profound paths in the *Myriad Veil Cosmos*: the **Sword Dao**, embodiment of will and clarity, and the **Spatial Dao**, master of distance and dimension.

Cultivators of this hybrid Dao are known as **Veilcutters**, **Dimensional Bladesages**, or **Worldsplitters**. They do not just strike—they **rewrite space** with every swing, bypassing defense, delay, and even fate itself.

To follow the Spatial Sword Dao is to forge a blade that **transcends position**, turning the sword into a **vector of inevitability**.

---

## ⚔️ Core Tenets

- **No Distance, Only Intention**: The sword doesn’t travel. It *arrives*.
- **Cut the World, Not Just the Flesh**: Each slash severs fabric—of space, karma, or even time echoes.
- **Formless Positioning**: Mastery of space means never being where one is seen, and always striking from where none expect.

## 🏯 Known Masters & Sects

- **The Hollow Edge Sect**: Cultivators who live within a broken dimension known as the *Fractured Fold*. All their techniques bend the battlefield itself.
- **Jian Wuqian, the Veilpiercer**: Legend says he cut through a sealed realm to slay a beast god with a single swing, creating the *Voidscar Expanse*.
- **The Astral Seam Walkers**: Nomadic sword cultivators who cross realms through sword-carved tunnels. Known for the *Seven Rift Step* sword style.

## ⚠️ Weaknesses & Limitations

- **Mental Complexity**: The mind must calculate folds, vectors, and intent simultaneously. Failure leads to missed strikes or self-harm.
- **Tribulation Severity**: Fusion Daos invite **Heavenly Sword-Space Tribulations**, known to be reality-warping and spirit-devouring.
- **Dao Instability**: A cultivator must fully harmonize Sword and Space—divided will causes dangerous Qi dissonance or Dao Heart fracture.
