# 🩸 Desolate Baptisms (荒劫)

> *"To walk the Desolate Path is to be unmade—again and again—until only truthless will remains."*  
> —⟪Desolation Codex⟫, Fragment II

---

## 🔥 What Are the Desolate Baptisms?

**Desolate Baptisms** are the three transcendent trials that any aspirant must undergo to begin walking the **Path of Desolate Immortality**.

They are not rituals, nor tests imposed by others. They are *natural calamities* rooted in the Eight Desolates—primordial trials of unmaking that occur when the universe recognizes an individual who no longer belongs to its laws.

Failure does not kill the cultivator—it fractures them eternally across realms, leaving behind echoes, regrets, or walking corpses filled with false memories.

---

## 🕯️ The Three Baptisms

### 1. 🕳️ Time Collapse (时崩)
Occurs in the `Witherwind Expanse` or `Frozen Karma Wastes`.

> Time shatters around the cultivator. Moments blend and bleed. They must watch themselves die, forget, and exist again—across past, present, and non-linear futures.

- **Goal**: Abandon reliance on temporal cause-and-effect.
- **Survivor Trait**: Unbound from time delays (instantaneous perception).

---

### 2. 🌌 Dao Fracture (道碎)
Occurs in the `Echoing Grave of the Dao`.

> The cultivator’s core Dao is shattered in a confrontation with forgotten truths. All previous cultivation paths become invalid. Dao hearts rupture; meaning disintegrates.

- **Goal**: Let go of all beliefs and former enlightenment.
- **Survivor Trait**: Immune to Dao suppression and conceptual attacks.

---

### 3. 🧬 Reality Decay (界蚀)
Occurs in the `Ashen Veil` or `Oblivion Maw`.

> Physical, spiritual, and cognitive aspects erode. Identity fragments. The world begins to forget the cultivator exists, and the cultivator begins to forget themselves.

- **Goal**: Prove existence in the face of non-being.
- **Survivor Trait**: Cannot be fully erased or sealed.

---

## ☠️ Consequences of Failure

| Baptism         | Failure Result                                  |
|-----------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| Time Collapse   | Chrono-scattered soul shards                     |
| Dao Fracture    | Dao feedback loop—eternal enlightenment spiral   |
| Reality Decay   | Eternal semi-death; unable to reincarnate or die |

---

## 🗝️ Notes

- Baptisms must be survived *in order* and *alone*.
- Most Abyssal Immortals, Celestial Emperors, and Dao Monarchs cannot endure even the **first** baptism.
- Desolate Immortals often manifest strange phenomena afterward: no shadows, erased footprints, or voices that carry across dimensions.

> *"The Desolate Immortal is not made—they are what remains after everything else is stripped away."*
