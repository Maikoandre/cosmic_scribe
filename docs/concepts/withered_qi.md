# 🧬 Withered Qi (枯气)

> *"It is the breath of ruin. The echo of what should have been. It grants no life—but with it, one may unmake eternity."*  
> —From the torn pages of an Ash-Walker’s diary

---

## 🌫️ What is Withered Qi?

**Withered Qi** is a decayed, corrupted form of spiritual energy that naturally seeps through the **Eight Desolates**. It is what remains when Heaven's Qi dies—stripped of nurturing power, of resonance with the Dao, and of the ability to harmonize with creation.

This Qi:
- **Cannot support life**, plants, or beasts.
- **Poisons meridians** of regular cultivators.
- **Corrupts techniques** and alters spiritual artifacts.
- Yet to **Desolate Immortals**, it is *pure essence*—untainted by Samsara or Karmic Law.

---

## 🔥 Key Properties

| Property               | Description                                                                 |
|------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Nature                 | Anti-spiritual / Anti-conceptual                                            |
| Color & Scent          | Dull gray-green, faint scent of ash and metal                               |
| Behavioral Trait       | Absorbs rather than radiates energy                                         |
| Qi Movement            | Inward-folding; coils upon itself                                           |
| Interaction with Dao   | Incompatible — causes rejection or collapse of Dao patterns                 |
| Compatibility          | Only with Desolate Techniques, Desolate Immortals, or corpse-forging arts   |

---

## 🧪 Applications in Cultivation

- Used to **fuel Desolate Techniques**
- Enables survival during **Desolate Baptisms**
- Can **undo formations**, disrupt laws, or erase karmic bindings
- Infuses the **Desolate Codex**, a scripture readable only under its influence

> *"Withered Qi does not nourish—it unroots. In it, the Dao finds no purchase."*

---

## ☣️ Side Effects for Non-Desolate Cultivators

| Exposure Level     | Effect                                             |
|--------------------|----------------------------------------------------|
| Minor (1 breath)   | Qi deviation, minor hallucinations                 |
| Moderate (1 hour)  | Meridians shrivel; cultivation regression begins   |
| Major (1 day)      | Collapse of spiritual foundation; loss of Dao sense|
| Long-term exposure | Mutation into Wither-Walkers or spiritual rot      |
