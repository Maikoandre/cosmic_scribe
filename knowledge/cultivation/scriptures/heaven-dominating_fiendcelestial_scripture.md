🌌 Heaven-Dominating Fiendcelestial Scripture (霸天巨神诀)

Grade: Immortal
Type: Body Cultivation Martial Technique
Origin: Said to be inscribed on the bones of a slain Primordial Fiendcelestial who fought against the Heavens themselves. Only those with indomitable will and monstrous physical constitutions can even attempt to comprehend the first line.

---

📖 Description:

The Heaven-Dominating Fiendcelestial Scripture is not merely a body refinement art—it is a path to becoming a walking calamity. It reshapes the user's mortal shell into something beyond comprehension: a vessel capable of shouldering mountains, walking through tribulations unscathed, and striking fear into Celestial Beasts and Immortal Emperors alike.

This technique allows a cultivator to transcend the human form, gradually transforming their body into that of a Fiendcelestial Avatar, layer by layer, marrow to skin. It does not rely on Qi circulation alone—it fuses Heaven and Earth energy directly into the bones, blood, and sinew, enabling the user to regenerate, evolve, and ascend without bottleneck.


---

🔥 Stages of Cultivation:

1. Bronze Marrow Recasting (铜髓重铸)
The user crushes their own marrow through meditative pressure and replaces it with spiritual bronze essence. Pain beyond imagination is endured, but strength increases a hundredfold.


2. Star-Tendon Suturing (星筋缝合)
Tendons are fused with astral light drawn from constellations. Movements become like meteor strikes—every punch generates sonic booms and shockwaves.


3. World-Bone Forging (界骨锻体)
Bones become like small worlds—dense, layered, and able to store spiritual energy. One stomp can fracture mountains; one roar can rupture space.


4. Fiendcelestial Heart Awakening (巨神心觉)
A second heart forms, pulsating with pure Heaven-Earth energy. This allows the user to fight indefinitely, heal rapidly, and defy death itself unless the heart is destroyed.


5. Heaven-Dominating Avatar (霸天化身)
The user can manifest a Fiendcelestial Form that mirrors their own movements. The form stands hundreds of meters tall and is immune to all but Immortal-grade attacks.
In this state, the cultivator’s body becomes a divine weapon. A single finger-flick can erase nascent souls; a clap can shatter divine artifacts.




---

⚔️ Signature Techniques:

One Punch Flattens Nine Heavens (一拳平九天)
Channels all strength into a single punch that disregards distance, space, and realm boundaries. Used as a finishing move against Immortal foes.

Flesh Rebirth Sutra (血肉再生经)
As long as one drop of blood remains, the cultivator can regenerate entirely—even regrow lost limbs or organs instantly.

Heaven-Burying Step (埋天一踏)
A stomp that disrupts spiritual veins in the area, collapsing formations, sealing realms, or cutting off teleportation.



---

☯️ Cultivation Requirements:

Body must have survived at least ten Tribulation Lightnings bare-skinned.

Soul must be strong enough to remain conscious during marrow rebirth.

Forbidden for those with frail constitutions or reliant on Qi refinement alone.

Requires fusion of five Fiendcelestial Essences, which can only be extracted from ancient world remnants or slain divine beasts.



---

🩸 Side Effect / Risk:

Mastering this technique is akin to becoming a Force of Nature. Those who fail the body transformation stages suffer complete collapse, disintegration, or worst—eternal suffering trapped in their own mutilated flesh, unable to die, yet incapable of healing.
