# ❄️ Cold Star Secret Records (寒星秘录)

> *“Within every breath of frost lies a star unborn. To the patient hand, Yin births light.”*  
> — Preface, Cold Star Secret Records

---

## 📜 Overview

**Cold Star Secret Records** (寒星秘录) is an ancient cultivation art originating from the frigid domains beyond the Frozen Karma Wastes. It is famed for its ability to nurture **Lifesource Stars** by absorbing and refining **cold Yin Qi**, forming an internal constellation that empowers the cultivator's body and soul.

This secret art is rarely taught openly, as each star born through its method must be tempered by silence, solitude, and sub-zero environments. It is said that practitioners of this art become as cold and distant as the stars they summon.

---

## 🧊 Cultivation Method

- **Core Concept**: Transform Yin Qi into Lifesource Stars via internal meridian rotation and soul tempering.
- **Required Environment**:  
  - High-purity Yin Qi zones (glacial tombs, karmic glaciers, shadowed mountain pits)  
  - Isolation and prolonged exposure to spiritual cold
- **Main Meridian Focus**:  
  - Lunar Meridian (月脉)  
  - Frost Vein Channels (霜脉)

---

## 🌌 Effects

- Each cultivated **Cold Star** enhances:  
  - **Soul Stability**  
  - **Cold-type Resistance & Affinity**  
  - **Astral Sensing Abilities**
- Advanced cultivators can manifest **Starfire Frost Qi**, capable of freezing spiritual flames and illusions alike.
- Multiple Cold Stars may orbit the dantian and soul core, forming an internal **Constellation Domain**.

---

## ☠️ Warnings

- Absorbing Yin Qi too quickly can lead to **Frozen Meridian Syndrome**, rendering cultivation paths inert.
- Practitioners may become emotionally distant, with rumors of some losing their ability to feel warmth—spiritual or emotional.

---

## 🧭 Known Practitioners & Lineages

- **The Glacier-Blood Queen** – Said to have cultivated nine Cold Stars, achieving near-undying soul resonance.
- **Frozen Sky Monastery** – A sect that guards the secret continuation volumes of this art.
- **Ashen Phoenix Sect** (dissident branch) – Rumored to have hybridized the Cold Star Records with rebirth flame techniques.
