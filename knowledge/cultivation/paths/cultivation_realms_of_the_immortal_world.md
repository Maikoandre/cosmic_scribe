The journey to immortality is divided into distinct realms, each marking a profound transformation of body, spirit, and soul.

---

## 1. **Qi Refinement Realm (气炼境)**
> The beginning of the path. Cultivators absorb spiritual energy into their bodies, tempering their flesh and meridians. A stage of internal cultivation and building a foundation for higher realms.

---

## 2. **Foundation Establishment Realm (筑基境)**
> Solidifying the spiritual core. Cultivators form a stable foundation, allowing the storage and circulation of greater spiritual power. Marks the end of the mortal body’s limits.

---

## 3. **Golden Core Realm (金丹境)**
> The birth of the Golden Core. Energy condenses into a powerful core within the dantian, granting increased longevity and supernatural strength. The cultivator’s essence begins to change.

---

## 4. **Essence Soul Realm (元婴境)**
> Also known as the Nascent Soul stage. The soul separates from the body and can act independently. Cultivators can perform soul projection and survive without their physical form.

---

## 5. **Void Refinement Realm (炼虚境)**
> The cultivator begins merging with the void, refining their soul and spiritual power into pure essence. Reality itself becomes malleable to their will.

---

## 6. **Loose Immortal Realm (人仙境)**
> The first step into true immortality. Mortality is shed, and the cultivator attains a lifespan spanning millennia. Possesses limited control over worldly laws.

---

## 7. **True Immortal Realm (真仙境)**
> A realm of transcendent power. The cultivator's body and soul are fully immortal, untouched by time or decay. Capable of controlling spiritual laws with ease.

---

## 8. **Heaven Immortal Realm (天仙境)**
> The cultivator resonates with the heavens. Capable of calling upon the celestial forces, they become guardians or rulers of heavenly domains.

---

## 9. **Celestial Immortal Realm (玄仙境)**
> A realm beyond comprehension. Cultivators perceive and manipulate the fundamental laws of existence. Often serve as envoys of higher celestial orders.

---

## 10. **Dao Immortal Realm (道仙境)**
> The cultivator becomes one with the Dao. They no longer follow the laws of the universe—they are the law. Words and thoughts shape reality.

---

## 11. **Sage Realm (圣人境)**
> The pinnacle of cultivation. A being of boundless wisdom and omnipotent presence. Their existence influences entire planes, and their actions echo across time.

---
