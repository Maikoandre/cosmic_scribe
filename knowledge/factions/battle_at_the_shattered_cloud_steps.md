# ⚔️ Battle at the Shattered Cloud Steps  
**Golden Immortal Showdown: Hollow Edge vs. Crimson Veil**

---

> *"To touch the disciples of Hollow Edge is to provoke the blade of emptiness itself." — Elder Xuanzhen of the Hollow Edge Sect*

---

## 🌫️ Setting

The battle unfolds atop the **Shattered Cloud Steps**, a floating ruin of ancient immortal stone steps suspended over a Void Sea. Once a holy site for Dao pilgrimage, it now echoes with war cries and resonating Dao intent.

Disciples of the Hollow Edge Sect, returning from a mission in the Middle Immortal Domain, are intercepted by **Crimson Veil Elder Mò Yeying**, known for his ruthless extermination of rival legacies. He seeks the **Folded Mirror Compass** in their possession.

---

## 💥 The Clash

The first strike is merciless.

Elder Mò Yeying's crimson veil unfurls like a tidal shroud, enshrouding the steps in suffocating scarlet mist. From within, countless spirit-killing threads of blood Qi lash out at the young disciples.

> “Your Hollow Edge blades mean nothing in my domain of the Heart-Weeping Blood Mist.”

Cries ring out—but not for long. A soundless pulse silences the scene.

The mist is carved apart.

Emerging through a rift in space itself, **Elder Xuanzhen of Hollow Edge Sect** arrives. Robes black as night, edged with starlight thread, he draws a blade that reflects nothing—**Voidtrace**, a Dao Weapon of the **Spatial Sword Path**.

> “You should have killed them quickly. Now you've invited emptiness.”

### ✴️ Dao Clash: Spatial Sword vs. Crimson Blood Mist

- **Mò Yeying** activates his **Crimson Heart Sutra**, summoning a phantasmal palace of blood atop the battlefield.
- **Xuanzhen** draws no blade—he simply steps, vanishing from space.

Crimson chains surge forth, seeking to bind time and intent itself. But they are severed mid-trajectory.

> A **Folded Step** — time and space recoil.

Xuanzhen reappears behind Mò Yeying, who barely raises his defenses as a single **Silent Cut** passes through his spiritual defenses.

His left arm disintegrates into folded space, leaving behind spatial fissures that pulse with Dao rejection.

> “You… you sever the Heavens so easily…”

> “I sever illusions of permanence,” Xuanzhen replies, his voice calm, distant.

Desperate, Mò Yeying unleashes his **Bloodline Burning Art**, setting his immortal veins ablaze to fuel a final strike: a blood-drenched lotus blooming mid-air, meant to seal all senses and cut away memory.

Xuanzhen answers by raising a single finger.

### 🌌 Finishing Technique: **Hollow Severance – Return to Stillness**

A ripple distorts the air—then the lotus, the blood, the sound, and even light itself are consumed by an invisible edge. Everything halts. Then, in a breath, collapses inward and vanishes.

Mò Yeying is gone. Not slain—**unmade**.

---

## ⚖️ Aftermath

The disciples, shaken but alive, kneel in respect. Xuanzhen sheathes Voidtrace into the void itself.

> “Let this be remembered: the Hollow Edge is not dulled by time, only hidden within it.”

The Shattered Cloud Steps fall silent once more, but echoes of the clash will ripple through the Immortal World.

---

**Casualties:**  
- Crimson Veil Elder Mò Yeying – annihilated.  
- Hollow Edge Disciples – 2 injured, none dead.

**Legacy Impact:**  
- Hollow Edge Sect's prestige surges.  
- Crimson Veil Alliance marks Elder Xuanzhen for "observation."
