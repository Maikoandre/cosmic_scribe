## 🏞️ Origins
The Hollow Edge Sect was not founded by proclamation, but by **absence**.

- The founder, whose name was **erased from causality**, retreated into the **Fractured Fold Range**.
- From this mysterious region rose a sect that rejected form, fame, and structure.
- Their earliest teachings were not etched into stone, but into **folded gaps in reality**.

> *"To follow the Hollow Edge is to abandon the world’s rhythm. We cut only where the Dao folds."*  
> — Founder's Echo, Fragment 17

---

## 🕋 Sect Structure
- **Headquarters:** Believed to be hidden in a **Folded Mirror Garden**, suspended in unmoving reality.
- **Visibility:** The sect does not maintain open halls, banners, or territory.
- **Recruitment:** Unknown. Possibly karmic or fate-bound selection.
- **Presence:** Often leaves behind only:
  - Clean, bloodless corpses.
  - Spatial lacerations.
  - Nothing at all.

---

## ⚔️ Cultivation Style
- **Doctrine:** Emphasis on **absence**, **subtlety**, and **Dao-fold manipulation**.
- **Techniques Include:**
  - `Folded Presence Art`
  - `Hollow Trace Step`
  - `Void-Diverging Edge` (Severs perception and space)
- **Signature Weapon:**
  - *Fracture of the Second Sky* — A **Forged Tribulation Dao Weapon** aligned with Spatial Intent.
  - Last seen: Eight epochs ago.

---

## 🧭 Political Interaction
- **Alliances:** None known.
- **Participation:** Does not attend sect convocations or alliances.
- **Historical Intervention:**
  - During the **Third Starfall Crisis**, a crimson sword light cleaved a comet catastrophe in two.
  - All witnesses perished—except one boy who later founded the *Mirrorwatching School*.

> *"The sword came, then the sky bent. I think they watched us all along."*  
> — Li Feiyan, Founder of Mirrorwatching School

---

## 🧠 Sect Philosophy
- **Central Idea:** True power lies not in the blade’s swing, but in **where it is not**.
- **Tactical Superiority:** Illusion, gap exploitation, presence erasure.
- **Dao Expression:** Folded reality, absence as strike, precision over power.
